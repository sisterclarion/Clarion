A New Jamaica of Peace

A New Jamaica started as a feeding programme for the homeless on the streets of Kingston Jamaica. Sister Clarion would prepare meals at home and go into the highways and byways to share with her fellow Jamaicans in a spirit of unconditional love and peace. Accompanied by her nineteen year old daughter Hatshepsut and brother Shumba, Sister Clarion thinks "it is important to encourage acts of kindness in our youth in order to create a better society"; A New Jamaica of Peace. In September of 2015 A New Jamaica held its first Fun Day in the innercity community of Arnett Gardens also known as Trench Town made popular by the late Bob Marley.


Howard University's Ambassadors of Engineering partner with A New Jamaica of Peace Foundation to teach game coding to students at Jessie Ripoll Primary on May 29,2018.
